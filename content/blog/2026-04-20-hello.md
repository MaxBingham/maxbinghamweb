I do not have readers yet, and that is the right time to start a personal site. The only job of this first post is to exist: the archive should not be empty while the real notes are still in my head.

A few rules I am giving myself:

- Keep posts short enough that I actually want to write the next one.
- Prefer being specific over being clever.
- Never apologize for slow posting — just post.

If any of this aged badly in six months, good, that means I learned something.
